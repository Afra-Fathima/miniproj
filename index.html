WE ARE ONE BIG HAPPY FAMILY

The Megha Foundation is a non-governmental organization supported by donations, member contributions, and some local assistance. The Megha Foundation was established in August 1997 under the Societies Registration Act 1974 with the goal of facilitating the development of orphan children into responsible and respectable citizens by providing an environment suitable to their growth and development.

Since its establishment, the Megha Foundation has worked to improve the lives of destitute children and women, particularly in Chennai's slums and rural Kanjeepuram.

The Saranalaya shelter home of The Megha Foundation provides warmth, care, food, clothes, shelter, Medicare recreation, education, and vocational training for free. More than 15 students have graduated from Saranalaya.
